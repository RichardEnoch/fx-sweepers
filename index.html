About Fx Sweepers

Sweeping Nigeria forward

A modern cleaning company on a mission to bring the Nigerian outdoor-cleaning industry to a world-class stage.

Who we are

A registered company built for scale

Fx Sweepers is a registered limited liability company providing commercial outdoor sweeping nationwide. We operate a fleet of electric, machine-powered sweepers — treating cleaning as infrastructure, delivered to a consistent, measurable standard.

From a single forecourt to a portfolio of estates and malls, we have the equipment, the process and the reach to keep your spaces immaculate.

What we do

Machine-powered sweeping & equipment sales

Two sides of the same expertise. We run managed sweeping contracts across commercial spaces, and we supply the commercial machinery — ride-on sweepers, scrubber-driers and walk-behind units — to organisations that want to clean in-house.

Either way, the result is the same: world-class clean, powered by electric machines, not manual labour.
